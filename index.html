Dental Veneers

Dental veneers are thin, custom-made shells of tooth-colored material placed over the front surface of teeth to improve their appearance, correcting issues like chipped, stained, or crooked teeth Veneers are used in cosmetic dentistry to correct a variety of dental issues, including:
Discolored Teeth: Fixing severe discoloration or uneven coloring that can't be addressed with whitening.
Chipped or Broken Teeth: Restoring the appearance of teeth that have been damaged.
Misaligned or Worn Teeth: Improving the shape and appearance of teeth that are worn down or oddly shaped.
Between Teeth:Closing gaps to create a more uniform smile.

Dental Implants

WHAT EXACTLY ARE DENTAL IMPLANTS ?
A Dental Implant is actually a titanium post surgically inserted into the jawbone beneath the gum line which work as a tooth root. After insertion, a dental implantologist will attach the crown on top of the implant to ensure that the teeth appear natural.

The best part about tooth implants is that they not only look or feel like your natural teeth but function like them as well. Get the top dental implant services in Faridabad only at Dr. Dipti's Smile Suite.
